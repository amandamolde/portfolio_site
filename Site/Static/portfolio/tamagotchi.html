Tamagotchi

JavaScript, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS

Tamagotchi is a browser-based remake of the handheld digital pet relased in May 1997. This virutal pet gets hungry, sleepy, and bored. If any of the progress bars fill entirely, the pet will pass away. Be sure to click the Feed Me, Lights Out, or Let's Play buttons to decrease your pet's Hunger, Boredom, or Sleepiness. Your pet will morph as he or she gets older.

Tamagotchi was my first front-end project. I used jQuery to manipulate the DOM and Bootstrap to style the page. I found a great source for plug and play animations at Animate.css.
